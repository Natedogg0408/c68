campus life

In Grass Valley you begin school at 8:30 am (kids who arrive early can play on the playground whilst they wait). Once you are in the school you head to your (anywhere from kindergarden to fifth grade) homeroom, which would be math and science or English language arts. After lunch, classes are swapped. On normal days you get out of school at 2:45 but on Wednesdays you get out at 12:30. For advanced students every thursday get an hour of advanced stem learning including advanced math, coding robots or games, building the robots, and much much more. Every month (it is random within the month) we will have a surprise fire drill.
